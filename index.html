Dr. Roseline Oluwaseun Ogundokun

Lecturer in Computer Science, Researcher, and Scholar

Education

  • BSc in Management Information Systems, Covenant University, Ota
  • MSc in Computer Science, University of Ilorin
  • PhD in Computer Science, University of Ilorin
  • PhD in Multimedia Engineering (in progress), Kaunas University of Technology

Research and Academic Contributions

Dr. Ogundokun has contributed significantly to the fields of computer vision, deep learning, medical imaging, image processing, steganography and cryptography, information security, and artificial intelligence. She has authored approximately 131 articles indexed in SCOPUS and 171 publications in Google Scholar and Web of Science (WoS). Her collaborative efforts span globally, involving over 50 co-authors.

Recognition and Rankings

Dr. Ogundokun is ranked 28th in Nigeria as per SCOPUS-Elsevier data (June 2023), progressing significantly from her earlier rankings. Her consistent growth and influence are evident, previously ranked 23rd in 2022, 50th in 2021, and 175th in 2020.

Professional Focus

Dr. Ogundokun is committed to advancing knowledge and innovation in computer science through teaching, research leadership, and mentorship.

Future Directions

She aims to complete her PhD in Multimedia Engineering and expand her research portfolio with impactful studies that contribute to the global advancement of computer science and technology.
