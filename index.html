About Me

Dr. Roseline Oluwaseun Ogundokun is a lecturer in Computer Science at the College of Pure and Applied Sciences, Landmark University, Omu Aran, Kwara State, Nigeria. She holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science and is currently pursuing a second Ph.D. in Multimedia Engineering at Kaunas University of Technology, Lithuania.

Education

  • Bachelor of Science in Management Information Systems - Covenant University, Ota
  • Master of Science in Computer Science - University of Ilorin
  • Ph.D. in Computer Science - University of Ilorin
  • Ph.D. in Multimedia Engineering (Ongoing) - Kaunas University of Technology

Research Areas

Dr. Ogundokun's research areas include computer vision, deep learning, medical imaging, image processing, steganography and cryptography, information security, and artificial intelligence.

Publications

Dr. Ogundokun has made notable contributions to the academic world with approximately 131 published articles indexed in SCOPUS and 171 publications in Google Scholar and Web of Science (WoS). She has collaborated globally with over 50 coauthors.
